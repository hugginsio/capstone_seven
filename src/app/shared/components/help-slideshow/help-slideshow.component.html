Game Pieces

Minin' Sites make up the entire mine and represent which gems y’all can collect from the site and just how many gems it has to give according to the number in the site

These here tracks are how y’all traverse the mine. Each of a players’ tracks must be adjacent to another of their tracks.

Mining Markers are placed on the corner of a mining site where y’all want to mine for gems. These have to be connectin’ to one of your rail tracks in the mine.

Startin' the Game

Each of y’all gets 2 pairs of your team’s mining markers and tracks at the start of the duel.

Miner 1 places one axe and a connecting track for the starting turn anywhere in the mine.

To keep things fair and square we do a little do-si-do in the set up turns like we explained above, rotating from Miner 1 to Miner 2, Miner 2 again, then Miner 1

Helpful Hints

Only in these startin’ turns can your pair be placed not in connection with the other pair.

If y’all ever be regrettin’ your turn, just hit undo!

Once you’ve settled your mind a bit, just hit “End Turn” to carry on the game.

Start of Each Turn

Let the games begin! Now y’all have freedom to place whatever you can afford in the mine. Rotatin’ back n forth and hittin’ “End Turn” when you’re finished.

Now, before you start making moves on your turn, your hard work will start to pay off. At the start of each turn you get your earnings of one gem from each site you have a mining marker touchin’.

Building Your Operation

Now that y’all got two startin’ points to your mining operation you can build rails and place mining markers off either of your startin’ points.

Other miners can their mining markers in your path but your rails’ve gotta connect.

All Tuckered Out

Now them gems is limited in our mine here and only a certain amount can be mined per turn, as indicated by the number of gems present on each site.

If y’all be tryin’ to corner a minin’ site by placing more mining markers than gems on a site, then that site is what we folks in the business call “tuckered out”. Y’all both cain’t be gettin any more gems from that site now.

Y’all can still be puttin’ markers down around the “tuckered out” site but you cain’t get gems from that site.

Claimin' a Site

Stake your claim for exclusive minin’ rights by formin’ a perimeter of your rail track around one or more sites!

Once you’ve completely surrounded a site, only your business can mine there. Others can put a marker or two down but they cain’t get any gems from your site or try to cut across your site. No sir, no opponent tracks allowed here!

Best of all, now that you have claimed a site there’s no chance to tucker it out! Put as many minging markers as you can fit and it’ll still be giving you a gem for each touchin' marker on each turn.

How To Win

First miner to 10 points wins!

+1 for each mining marker placed

+1 for each site you claim

+2 for the miner with the longest set of connected rails in the mine

MasterMine

Howdy, pardners! Welcome to the MasterMines.

Miner and Machine are facin' off to get rich quick by makin' Paths and pickin' Sites full of Gems.

Click on this here "Guided Tutorial" button to join a duel and learn the ropes while ya play, or click "Next" to just keep on readin'.

Start Guided Tutorial

Game Pieces

Sites are all over the mine and tell ya which of them Gems can be mined there. Each Site'll also tell ya how many Tools it can have on its corners.

These here Paths are how y’all traverse the mine. Ya can only lay down a Path if it's next to a Path ya already placed.

Tools are placed on a Site's corners and must be connectin' to one of your Paths.

For each of them Tools you got on a Site's corners, you'll nab one of its Gems at the start of each turn.

Buyin' Tools and Paths

Startin' the Game

Each of y’all gonna get 2 sets of Tools and Paths at the start of a duel.

Player 1 places a Tool and a connecting Path anywhere they want to start us off.

After that, to keep things fair and square, we do a little do-si-do where Player 2 takes two turns before Player 1 gets to go again.

Helpful Hints

Only in these startin’ turns can your Tools and Paths not be connectin' with other Tools and Paths.

If y’all ever be regrettin’ your turn, just hit "Undo!"

Once you’ve settled your mind, hit “End Turn” to let your opponent have a go.

All Tuckered Out

Now ya can't be havin' more Tools on a Site than there are Gems. If ya do, that Site is gonna get "Tuckered Out" and ain't nobody gettin' Gems from it, no matter how many Tools they got on its corners.

Claimin' a Site

Stake your claim for exclusive minin’ rights by surroundin' Sites with your Paths!

If you surround a Site, only you can mine it. Others can put their Tools to work there, but they won't be gettin' any Gems.

Best of all, a surrounded Site can't be Tuckered Out!

Tradin' Post

Missin' the right Gems? Head on down to the Trading Post!

Trade any combination of 3 Gems for 1 Gem of any other type.

Now don't get too greedy! You can only make 1 Trade per turn.

How To Win

The first of y'all to 10 points wins!

Ya get 1 point for each Tool placed.

Ya get 1 point for each Site you surround.

Ya get 2 points if ya have the longest set of connectin' Paths.

Settin' Up a Competition

Pick your Minin' Background

Click on the buttons to sort between all our nifty options for your duel.

Want to make your own mine?

Enter some blueprints just like the ones below, separated by commas, and make sure each o’ them letter/number pairings come up just once in the list.

R1, R2, R3, B1, B2, B3, G1, G2, G3, Y1, Y2, Y3, 00

Newfangled "On The Line" Technology

Here y'all get to duel with folks from all over.

Want to start the duel yourself? Click "Host Game" and pick them there settings like you'd normally do.

Now just sit back and wait 'til somebody comes along to accept your challenge.

Options Menu

Visit the "Menu" to look at all our nifty features, where you can change the look and sound of your mine, free of charge!

Forget how somethin' works in the middle of a duel? Mosey on down to the "Help" button in the top left corner of your screen.

Giving up already? Go on an' git via this here emergency exit shaft.

Looks like you're all ready to go! Good luck, pardner!
